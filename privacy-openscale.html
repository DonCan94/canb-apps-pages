Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

OpenScale is developed and operated by CanB Apps ("we", "our", or "us").

Health Data

OpenScale integrates with Apple Health using the HealthKit framework to read and/or write health-related data such as body weight and body composition metrics.

This data is accessed only with your explicit permission through Apple's Health permissions system.

Health data accessed through HealthKit:

  • Is used solely to display and manage your weight tracking data within the app
  • Is stored locally on your device
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is never sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes

Information We Collect

OpenScale does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to the developer or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

OpenScale does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device and managed through Apple’s system frameworks such as Apple Health where applicable.
